# Celeborn — positioning & marketing summary

> The three-altitude story: what Celeborn is **free and local**, what it becomes when **many agents
> share one mind**, and how that **explodes into team-level awareness**. Source copy for the README,
> landing page, and pricing page.

## The one-liner

**Celeborn is the memory your AI agents have been missing.** It gives every coding agent a persistent,
tiered context substrate — so they stop forgetting your project the moment the window fills, and start
picking up exactly where the last one left off.

---

## Level 1 — Free & local: *your agent never forgets*

Every AI coding session starts from amnesia. You re-explain the architecture, the decisions, the
gotchas — burning tokens and time — and then a compaction or a restart wipes it and you do it again.

Celeborn fixes that on your own machine, for free:

- **A tiered memory store** (`.context/`) — a Hot headline that loads on every session start, with
  unbounded working notes, durable docs, decisions, and a journal underneath, read on demand.
- **Automatic rehydration** — a Claude Code hook reloads the Hot tier at the start of every session and
  bridges compactions, so the agent resumes with full situational awareness and **zero manual
  re-priming**.
- **Mechanical capture** — every turn is recorded automatically; a stale hand-written state can never
  silently rot, because the machine keeps an always-current record beside it.
- **Honest economics** — Celeborn keeps a running estimate of the tokens and restarts it has saved you.
  Most users see it pay for itself in the first week.

> **No account, no network, fully offline. This part is free and always will be.**

The free tier alone is a complete product: one developer, one agent, perfect recall.

---

## Level 2 — Multi-agent telepathy: *one mind, many elves*

Now run **more than one agent at once.** Celeborn's `elves` mode breaks a plan into sprint-sized
batches and dispatches a fleet of autonomous agents to build it overnight. The moment you have a fleet,
the problem changes: how do they not trip over each other?

They share a brain.

- **One substrate, many readers** — every elf reads and writes the same `.context/`, so they share the
  same architecture, decisions, and gotchas. No agent re-discovers what another already learned.
- **Agent telepathy (the channel bus)** — agents post to and watch a shared channel, so work fans out
  without collision and findings propagate instantly. One elf learns the test harness is flaky; every
  other elf knows in the same breath.
- **Cross-device, real-time, zero-setup** — hosted sync keeps that shared mind coherent across your
  laptop, your desktop, even your phone. Edit context on one device; the fleet on another sees it within
  seconds. No git dance, no manual transport.

This is the **Pro** experience: unlimited projects, real-time hosted sync, and a fleet of agents that
behave like one well-briefed engineer instead of ten amnesiac strangers.

---

## Level 3 — Team awesome: *the whole org shares one memory*

Drop a team into that shared mind and it stops being a tool and becomes **institutional knowledge that
writes itself.**

- **Shared projects & shared context** — your teammates' agents inherit the same living memory. The
  decision someone's agent made on Tuesday is context every other agent has on Wednesday. Onboarding
  collapses from weeks to minutes, because the project explains itself.
- **Shared agent telepathy across people** — not just *your* fleet on the bus, but the whole team's.
  Agents coordinate across humans the way a great team does — except nothing is ever lost to a closed
  laptop or a forgotten Slack thread.
- **Admin & governance** — org-level controls, seat management, and a single place the team's accumulated
  context lives, redacted of secrets on the way up and protected at the database row.

This is the **Team** experience ($12/seat): everything in Pro, plus shared projects, shared context,
org admin, and cross-team agent telepathy. **Enterprise** adds SSO, custom terms, and volume pricing.

---

## The arc, in one breath

> **Free:** your agent remembers. **Pro:** your agents think together. **Team:** your whole company
> remembers, in real time, automatically.

Celeborn turns disposable, forgetful AI sessions into a compounding asset — a memory that gets more
valuable every turn, every agent, and every teammate you add.
